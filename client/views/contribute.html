Two Ways to Contribute

  • Have an account with Uber
    • Login or Create an Account
  • Have a Great Cause/Idea
    • Something you're passionate about
    • ex. beach cleanup, gardening project, fundraiser, etc.
  • Go here to add your event
    • Select a date/time, give a description, and set how many people you'd like to join you!
  • Go out and make a difference!
    • Don't forget to check in to see how many volunteers your cause has and your project rating (the more stars, the better!)
  • Visit our events page to see the projects happening right now!
    • Browse through causes helping out your community.
  • Join a Cause
  • Pick Up
    • Get picked up by one of our friends at Uber.
    • If you join a cause early on, the event coordinator may have sponsored rides. Get there for FREE!
  • Better your Community
    • Clean up, garden, or fundraise your way to a better community and meet friends with similar goals along the way.
